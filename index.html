Why are otters so special?

They Hold Hands

Sea otters hold hands while sleeping in groups called "rafts" so they don't drift apart in the ocean currents.

They Have Built-in Pockets

Otters have a loose pouch of skin to store food and their absolute favorite rocks for cracking open shells.
